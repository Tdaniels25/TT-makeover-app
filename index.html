Interactive Mood Boards

This feature allows users to create custom mood boards for their design projects. Users can select images from a library of home design inspiration, add notes and comments, and organize their ideas into a cohesive visual layout. Users can share their mood boards with designers, contractors, or friends and family for feedback and collaboration.

Customized Reminders

To-Do Lists

This feature helps users stay on track with their home makeover projects by providing customized reminders and to-do lists. Users can set reminders for upcoming appointments, deadlines, and deliveries, and the app will send notifications to keep them informed. Users can also create and manage to-do lists for each project phase, such as demolition, construction, or decorating, to ensure that all tasks are completed on time.
