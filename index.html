    How it Works

    The app works by providing an interface where customers can post their renovation projects and receive quotes and proposals from various home builders.

    To use the app, customers first create an account and provide details about their renovation project, such as the scope of work, budget, and timeline. The app then matches the project with relevant home builders who have experience and expertise in the specific type of renovation work ., required.

    Home builders who are interested in the project can submit proposals and quotes through the app, outlining their proposed timeline, budget, and scope of work. Customers can then compare the different proposals and select the one that best meets their needs.

    Once the customer selects a home builder, they can communicate directly through the app to finalize the details of the project, such as the start date and payment terms. The app also provides a secure payment system, allowing customers to pay for the renovation services directly through the platform.

    Throughout the project, the app provides a project management tool that enables both the customer and the home builder to track the progress of the renovation work, communicate about any issues or changes, and ensure that the project is completed on time and within budget.
